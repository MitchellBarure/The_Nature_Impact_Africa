About Us

We empower businesses to understand, report, and reduce their environmental impact with smart, data-driven sustainability solutions.

Our Foundation & Focus

Nature Impact was founded in 2023 in Kigali, Rwanda as a youth-led progressive organization dedicated to sustainable development across Africa.

We specialize in Environmental Protection through Environmental Impact Assessments (EIAs), Environmental Management Plans (EMPs), and eco-friendly project designs.

Our approach merges human expertise with cutting-edge technological models and systems.

Our Approach

From scattered data to strategic decisions

Businesses today face increasing pressure from investors and regulators to measure, report, and reduce their impact on nature.

Nature Impact helps businesses quickly make sense of their sustainability data across operations and supply chains.

Our platform empowers companies to make strategic decisions that reduce environmental impact and drive progress towards sustainability goals.

Our Vision

To provide sustainable development designs and systems across the economic spectrum in Rwanda and across the African region

Our Mission

Provide affordable and reliable services for all through:
  • An AI Consultant
  • Nature & Environmental Assessments
  • Alignment to the Kunming Montreal Agreement on Biodiversity Framework
  • Environmental Planning
